Dario & Sara
Sensual Bachata
Spain
Darío and Sara started dancing together in 2015. Darío began working as a professor with just 17 years in Tenerife and Sara 3 years ago; They are 26 and 21 years old. Darío moved to Seville to dance with her. In her first year as a dance companion competing in various competitions in Spain. 1 year ago they built their own school in Seville called Sociale Danzi, this name made him because of his thought on the bachata, they think that is first of all a social dance. Darío and Sara are known for their different and original style and each day they have international recognition. In their workshop you will learn and also have a lot of fun with them. They like having a good dancing with the whole world!
